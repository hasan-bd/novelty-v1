We often create interior architecture or preserve existing elements that can be exposed and give character to space. We use a variety of presentation techniques throughout our projects to describe our work, each appropriately suited for the phase and task.

From Exterior to Interior

From interior to exterior, we at NEC always got your back. Interior work is part of our everyday lives and dates back to prehistoric times. Every individual experience and interacts with the built environment daily. Architecture is not only aesthetically pleasing to our minds and senses, but it also serves a purpose. Historian and Roman architect Marcus Vitruvius believes that architecture provides “firmness, commodity, and delight.” (Botturi 2007) Interiors are an aspect of our lives, and they are in our homes, workplaces, restaurants, shopping centers, schools, and hospitals.

The Right Use of Space

A right and very much arranged interior design can ensure that the space of a house or an office will be useful. An ineffectively designed structure may be monstrous, yet it may not be as practical as it should be. In the interim, a small area can be constructed in such a manner that it seems spacious and provides conveniences in its functionality.

Functionality can be accomplished with the assistance of the most recent design standards and components. Our expert interior designers ought to guarantee that their planned designs support social functions in the ideal manner conceivable.
